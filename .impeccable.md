## Design Context

### Users
Gym-goers and weight lifters who need precise rest timers between sets. They use the app mid-workout — phone on a bench or nearby — so every screen must be readable at a glance from arm's length. Primary audience is Chinese-speaking (zh_CN) with English support. They chose this app specifically because it's privacy-first: all data stays local, no cloud, no accounts, no social pressure.

### Brand Personality
**Warm, Focused, Energetic.** The app feels approachable and motivating — not clinical, not overwhelming. It's a training companion that brings positive energy to the gym. Inspired by Nike Training Club's premium quality, but with a friendlier, warmer tone. The interface should make you want to train, not just track numbers.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Flat Vitality** — the established design system. Warm gradient backgrounds (amber, coral, mint, rose, sky) paired with a deep indigo accent (#1A237E). White circular buttons with dark icons. 10px progress rings. Decorative background circles (semi-transparent white, iPhone 5c inspired). Floating pill-shaped navigation bar (25px radius, 70x70 center timer button).

- **References**: Nike Training Club — for its premium quality, bold confidence, and clean design execution
- **Anti-references**: Busy fitness apps with social feeds, gamification, or cluttered dashboards. No glass morphism, no neon, no dark-mode-only aesthetics
- **Theme**: Light mode is primary. Dark mode variants planned for all 5 color schemes
- **Locale**: Chinese (zh_CN) primary — UI copy, comments, and data presentation default to Chinese

### Design Principles

1. **Focus-first**: Every element serves the training flow. No distractions, no decorative noise. If it doesn't help the user lift better, it doesn't belong.

2. **One-glance clarity**: Timer countdown, set count, and current status must be readable from arm's length during an active set. Large typography, high contrast, no ambiguity.

3. **Flat Vitality consistency**: Warm gradients + deep indigo accent (#1A237E) + white surfaces. No glow, no glass effects, no shadows beyond the nav bar. All colors flow through `AppThemeData` — never hardcoded.

4. **Warm energy**: Professional but approachable. Smooth transitions (200-300ms), warm color palette. The app should feel like a motivating training partner, not a sterile tool.

5. **Accessible by default**: High contrast text on all themes. Touch targets ≥ 44x44pt. Light mode currently meets these standards; dark mode variants must maintain them.
